# Google Stock Price Prediction using Recurrent Neural Networks

## Overview

This project uses a Recurrent Neural Network (RNN) to analyze historical Google stock prices and predict future trends.

The model learns patterns from past stock market data and attempts to forecast upcoming price movements.

---

## Problem Statement

Financial markets generate large amounts of time-series data.

The goal of this project is to explore whether deep learning models can identify patterns in historical stock prices and use them to make predictions.

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains historical Google stock market data including:

- Opening Price
- Closing Price
- High Price
- Low Price
- Trading Volume

The model primarily learns from historical price trends over time.

---

## Why RNN?

Unlike traditional machine learning models, Recurrent Neural Networks are designed to process sequential data.

This makes them suitable for:

- Stock prices
- Weather forecasting
- Sales prediction
- Speech recognition
- Time-series analysis
